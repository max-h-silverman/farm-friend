Farm Friend

Terms of Service

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Farm Friend is a volunteer-coordination service for small farms on Vashon Island, Washington. By participating you agree to the terms below.

The service

Farm Friend uses SMS to notify volunteers about upcoming farm shifts and surplus produce pickups, and to coordinate which volunteers will help. Participation is voluntary and free.

Opting in and out

You may opt in by texting JOIN to our Farm Friend number, by giving verbal consent to the coordinator, or by being nominated by a participating farmer who has obtained your verbal consent. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Message frequency varies; standard message and data rates from your carrier may apply.

No employment relationship

Volunteering through Farm Friend does not create an employment relationship between you and any farm or with Farm Friend itself. You participate as a volunteer at your own discretion.

No guarantee of safety or supervision

Farms hosting volunteer work are responsible for the safety of their premises and the suitability of work assigned to volunteers. Farm Friend coordinates communication but does not inspect farm premises or supervise volunteer activity. Use common sense; do not perform work you are not physically able or trained to do.

Acceptable use

Don't use Farm Friend to harass, spam, impersonate, or coordinate activity unrelated to legitimate volunteer farm support. Doing so will result in removal from the service.

Changes to the service

Farm Friend is a pilot program operated by Max Silverman. Features, availability, and these terms may change. Material changes will be announced via SMS to active participants.

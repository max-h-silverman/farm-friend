CONTENT DETAILS

Campaign Description
Vashon Island Growers Association (VIGA) operates VIGA Farm Friend to keep the VIGA farm-stand map fresh and useful. VIGA Farm Friend sends operational SMS messages for participating Vashon farmers and community members, including farmer onboarding texts, farm-stand inventory update drafts, publish confirmations, prompts to confirm or revise a listing, customer produce availability inquiry replies, stock-out report alerts to farmers, and related help or opt-out messages. Farmers own their published listings; customer stock-out reports do not change the map directly and are routed to the farmer for review. The public opt-in call to action is published at https://www.vigavashon.org/viga-farm-friend.

Opt In Workflow Description (Message Flow/Call To Action)
The public mobile opt-in call to action is published on the Vashon Island Growers Association website at this exact path: https://www.vigavashon.org/viga-farm-friend. The page says: "Text JOIN to +1 206-864-5326 (206-86-GLEAN) to receive SMS messages from VIGA Farm Friend about VIGA farm-stand listings, farm-stand inventory updates, produce availability inquiries, and stock-out reports on Vashon Island. Message frequency varies based on farm activity and your use of the service, usually 0-6 messages per week. Message and data rates may apply. Consent is not a condition of purchase or participation. Reply HELP for help. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Terms: https://www.vigavashon.org/farm-friend-privacy-terms. Privacy: https://www.vigavashon.org/farm-friend-privacy-terms." The same JOIN call to action may also appear on VIGA Farm Friend outreach materials, VIGA website posts, VIGA social media posts, and in-person community or farmer outreach. Users opt in by texting JOIN or START to +1 206-864-5326. Farmers may also opt in during VIGA onboarding in person or by phone; VIGA records how and when SMS consent was received before any proactive SMS is sent. VIGA may review and approve a subscriber before VIGA Farm Friend sends operational messages.

## Squarespace Page: VIGA Farm Friend

# VIGA Farm Friend SMS

VIGA Farm Friend is operated by the Vashon Island Growers Association (VIGA) to keep the VIGA
farm-stand map fresh and useful. VIGA Farm Friend lets participating Vashon farmers update their farm
stand listings by SMS, helps community members ask about local farm-stand availability, and lets
customers report a likely stock-out so the farmer can review it.

VIGA Farm Friend messages are operational. They may include farmer onboarding texts, inventory
update confirmations, prompts to confirm or revise a farm-stand listing, customer inquiry replies,
stock-out report alerts to farmers, and related help or opt-out messages.

To request VIGA Farm Friend SMS access, text JOIN to +1 206-864-5326 (206-86-GLEAN).

By texting JOIN, you agree to receive SMS messages from VIGA Farm Friend about VIGA farm-stand
listings, farm-stand inventory updates, produce availability inquiries, and stock-out reports on
Vashon Island. Message frequency varies based on farm activity and your use of the service,
usually 0-6 messages per week. Message and data rates may apply. Consent is not a condition of
purchase or participation. Reply HELP for help. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.

VIGA may review and approve a subscriber before VIGA Farm Friend sends operational messages.
Farmers may also opt in during VIGA onboarding in person or by phone, where VIGA records how and
when SMS consent was received before sending any proactive SMS.

Your mobile opt-in information will not be sold or shared with third parties for promotional or
marketing purposes.

Terms: https://www.vigavashon.org/farm-friend-privacy-terms

Privacy: https://www.vigavashon.org/farm-friend-privacy-terms

Example messages:

- VIGA Farm Friend: Provo Farms currently lists kale, eggs, and tomatoes on the VIGA farm-stand
  map. Reply YES if still right, or text changes.
- VIGA Farm Friend: Draft update for Provo Farms: kale, eggs, tomatoes, and limited strawberries.
  Reply YES to publish to the VIGA farm-stand map or NO to cancel.
- VIGA Farm Friend: A customer reported Provo Farms may be out of kale. Reply OUT to mark it out,
  IGNORE to dismiss, or STOP to unsubscribe.

For questions, contact board@vigavashon.org.

## Squarespace Page: Privacy

# VIGA Farm Friend Privacy Policy

Effective date: [publish date]

VIGA Farm Friend is operated by the Vashon Island Growers Association (VIGA), P.O. Box 2894,
Vashon, WA 98070.

## Information We Collect

When you use VIGA Farm Friend SMS, we may collect your mobile phone number, SMS opt-in and
opt-out status, message history, HELP and STOP requests, farm-stand listing updates, produce
availability inquiries, stock-out reports, confirmation replies, and related operational records
needed to run the service.

## How We Use Information

We use this information to operate VIGA Farm Friend, send and manage SMS messages, keep the VIGA
farm-stand map current, respond to produce availability inquiries, route stock-out reports to the
appropriate farmer, honor HELP and STOP requests, review reported issues, protect the service from
misuse, and keep required operational and compliance records.

## SMS Privacy

Your mobile opt-in information will not be sold or shared with third parties for promotional or
marketing purposes.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional
purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this
information will not be shared with any third parties.

## Service Providers

VIGA may use service providers to operate the VIGA Farm Friend website, SMS delivery, hosting,
security, and support. These providers are permitted to use information only to provide services to
VIGA and not for their own marketing.

## Retention

We keep SMS and operational records only as long as needed to operate VIGA Farm Friend, honor
opt-out requests, resolve issues, meet legal or compliance obligations, and protect the service.

## Your Choices

You can unsubscribe from VIGA Farm Friend SMS at any time by replying STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL,
END, or QUIT. You can request help by replying HELP or INFO.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to board@vigavashon.org.

# VIGA Farm Friend SMS Terms

Effective date: [publish date]

VIGA Farm Friend SMS is operated by the Vashon Island Growers Association (VIGA), P.O. Box 2894,
Vashon, WA 98070.

## Program Description

VIGA Farm Friend sends operational SMS messages for the VIGA farm-stand map and related
farm-stand services on Vashon Island, Washington. Messages may include farmer onboarding texts,
farm-stand inventory update drafts, publish confirmations, prompts to confirm or revise a listing,
customer inquiry replies, stock-out report alerts to farmers, and related service messages.

## Opt In

You can request to opt in by texting JOIN or START to +1 206-864-5326 (206-86-GLEAN). VIGA may
review and approve a subscriber before VIGA Farm Friend sends operational messages. Farmers may
also opt in during VIGA onboarding in person or by phone, where VIGA records consent before any
proactive SMS is sent.

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on farm activity and your use of the service, usually 0-6 messages
per week.

## Costs

Message and data rates may apply. Consent is not a condition of purchase or participation.

## Opt Out

You can unsubscribe at any time by replying STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT. After you opt
out, VIGA Farm Friend will send one confirmation message and no further messages unless you
request to rejoin.

## Help

For help, reply HELP or INFO. You can also contact board@vigavashon.org.

## Supported Commands

Reply YES to confirm an update when a message asks for it. Reply NO to cancel a pending update when
a message asks for it. Reply OUT to mark a reported item out of stock when a farmer alert asks for
it. Reply IGNORE to dismiss a stock-out report. Reply FLAG to report an issue for VIGA review.
Reply STOP to unsubscribe.

## Privacy

VIGA Farm Friend handles SMS information according to the VIGA Farm Friend Privacy Policy:
https://www.vigavashon.org/farm-friend-privacy-terms
